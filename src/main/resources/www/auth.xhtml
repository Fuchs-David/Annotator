DBpedia annotator

This application is intended to provide means for volunteer annotators to mark DBpedia resources from the domain of bibliographic records by selecting appropriate category specified in Functional Requirements for Bibliographic Records (FRBR) developed by the International Federation of Library Associations and Institutions (IFLA). For details about FRBR entities, please follow either of these links depending on your preferred language:

  • Czech description of FRBR entities,
  • English description of FRBR entities.

When you create your account or log into an existing account, you can start annotating the DBpedia dataset at the main page.

Before starting annotating DBpedia, please take a look at the examples.
